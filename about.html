About VSC

The Vivekananda Study Circle (VSC) is a voluntary student initiative at IISc that promotes holistic development through dialogue, reflection, and service.

Inspired by Swami Vivekananda, VSC encourages students to engage with ideas related to science, society, culture, and values.
