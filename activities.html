Our Activities

Vivekananda Study Circle (VSC), IISc engages in reflective, service-oriented, and intellectually enriching activities inspired by the ideals of Swami Vivekananda.

Book Distribution Events

As part of National Youth Day and other occasions, VSC conducts book distribution drives across the IISc campus to spread inspiring literature on Swami Vivekananda, Indian philosophy, and values.

Cloth Collection Drives

VSC organizes cloth collection and distribution drives to support underprivileged communities. These initiatives are entirely student-led and emphasize compassion, dignity, and service.

Weekly Meetings & Study Circles

Weekly meetings form the heart of VSC activities. Members come together to read, reflect, and discuss the works of Swami Vivekananda and related philosophical texts, followed by open discussions.

Meeting notes and reflections are occasionally documented for continuity and for the benefit of new members.
