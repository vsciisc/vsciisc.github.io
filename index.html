Vivekananda Study Circle (VSC), IISc

Vivekananda Study Circle (VSC) is a student-led group at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), inspired by the ideals of Swami Vivekananda.

This website documents our activities, events, speakers, outreach programs, and alumni contributions, so that new students can learn about VSC even when earlier members have moved beyond campus.
